# Forward Tests

Every value on this page is on record ahead of the data or computation that
decides it. Each row carries a zero-dial OPH value, the sharpest current
measurement, the named future dataset that scores it, and the outcome that
kills its lane. The program's standing sentence is that the empirical record
contains zero landed, discriminating, frozen-prospective hits; this page lists
the places where that sentence can change.

Claim discipline: proximity to a measured value converts to evidence only at
an anchored registration with a frozen decision policy. Rows marked draft are
staged for anchoring and carry no verdict weight until the owner anchors them;
anchored artifacts live in
[`falsification/frozen_targets/`](../falsification/frozen_targets/). The full
shortlist with per-row status is the freeze-registry surface (FZ-01) pinned
from the signature-experiment tracker.

## Reading the table

Three properties hold for every row. The OPH value carries zero continuous
fit parameters; nothing in the theory layer can be adjusted toward the
measurement. The deciding data or computation does not exist at the time of
registration, so a landing scores as a forward result under the claim rules.
The kill condition is stated with the value, so a miss is a recorded death,
with failure permanence, rather than a relocation.

Two rows carry extra structure worth naming. The dark-energy row is doubly
exposed: the fixed-capacity theorem forces $w = -1$ identically, and the
drift map $w(a) = -1 + \tfrac{1}{3}\,d\ln N/d\ln a$ shows that mimicking the
DR2 thawing central would require capacity growth of order 84% per e-fold,
which the axiom forbids; the lane cannot bend toward the anomaly it is scored
against. The low-$\ell$ row inverts the usual anomaly hunt, and its scope is
exact. The screen null binds the screen's own contribution to the sky:
ordinary primordial fluctuations populate $\ell = 2, 3$ in OPH as in any
cosmology, and low quadrupole power is a scalar the symmetry never
constrains. What the icosahedral screen forbids is a deterministic imprint at
$\ell = 2..5$ and any anisotropic quadrupole covariance, since $\ell = 2$
restricts to the irreducible $\mathbf 5$ of $A_5$ and Schur forces isotropy.
What it permits is frame-locked octopole anisotropy through the
$\mathbf 3' \oplus \mathbf 4$ splitting, plus its fingerprint at $\ell = 6$
and, on the chiral screen, at $\ell = 15$, where no competing framework
places one. The observed quadrupole-octopole alignment is an a posteriori
temperature statistic with its axis near the ecliptic. Statistical isotropy
of the quadrupole is an assumption $\Lambda$CDM shares silently; a
polarization-confirmed cosmological alignment falsifies both frameworks, and
OPH alone registers that outcome as a kill condition in advance.
