# OPH Open Use And Anti-Patent Covenant

This file states the repository-wide patent policy for Observer Patch
Holography (OPH). It is intended to keep OPH mathematics, software,
real-world applications, devices, hardware designs, simulations, engineering
methods, experimental procedures, and implementation patterns available for
public study and implementation.

This is not legal advice. A formal legal review should harmonize this covenant
with the repository's copyright licenses and any future software, hardware, or
publication-specific licenses.

## Open Implementation Rule

Subject to the repository's copyright licenses for text, figures, code, data,
and other authored files, OPH material may be studied, implemented,
reproduced, modified, tested, deployed, manufactured, and shared.

This open implementation rule covers OPH-derived mathematics, algorithms,
software, simulations, hardware designs, experimental apparatus, control
methods, verification procedures, real-world applications, devices, and
engineering patterns described in this repository, the associated OPH papers,
and linked OPH publication and web surfaces.

## Anti-Patent Covenant

By using, adapting, implementing, distributing, deploying, manufacturing,
commercializing, or building on OPH material, you covenant not to file,
prosecute, maintain, acquire, assert, sell, license, threaten, or assist any
patent or patent claim whose claimed subject matter is OPH-derived.

A patent claim is OPH-derived if it depends on, incorporates, generalizes,
implements, applies, operationalizes, or would restrict others from practicing
OPH concepts, mathematics, algorithms, software, device architectures,
hardware methods, experimental methods, real-world applications, or
implementation patterns described in this repository, the associated OPH
papers, or linked OPH publication and web surfaces.

This covenant forbids using patents to create private monopolies over
OPH-derived ideas, devices, methods, systems, applications, software,
simulations, or engineering practice.

## Defensive Publications

This covenant does not forbid defensive publication, prior-art publication, or
other defensive steps whose sole purpose is to prevent patent enclosure of
OPH-derived subject matter, provided the defensive action irrevocably preserves
public freedom to study, implement, modify, manufacture, deploy, and share the
OPH-derived work.

## Loss Of Permission

Any person or entity that violates this covenant loses any repository-granted
permission to use, adapt, implement, distribute, deploy, manufacture,
commercialize, or build on OPH material to the maximum extent allowed by law.
That loss of permission applies to the violating person or entity and to any
affiliate, successor, assignee, patent holding company, or agent acting for the
same patent-enclosure purpose.

## No Warranty

OPH material is provided as-is, without warranty of any kind. No contributor
promises that OPH material is correct, safe, complete, fit for a particular
purpose, non-infringing, regulatory-approved, or suitable for deployment in any
device, experiment, product, service, or real-world system.

## Relationship To Other Licenses

Existing copyright licenses govern copying, redistribution, and adaptation of
authored files such as text, figures, code, datasets, and generated artifacts.
This file states the project's patent policy and open-use intent for
OPH-derived work. If a file-specific license grants broader rights, those
rights still carry this anti-patent covenant unless that file explicitly says
otherwise.
