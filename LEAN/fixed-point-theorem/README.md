# OPH Fixed-Point Theorem — Lean 4 Formalisation

Lean 4 / Mathlib formalisation of Proposition 4.2 and the Fixed-Point Corollary from:
B. Müller, "Paradise as Fixed-Point Consensus", May 2026.

## What is proved

- newman_lemma: Terminating + locally confluent → confluent (Newman's Lemma)
- unique_normal_form: Confluent relation has unique normal forms
- claim1_nf_is_fixedPt: Normal forms of T are fixed points of T
- descent_terminating: Descent potential Φ:X→ℕ implies termination
- deterministic_locally_confluent: Deterministic operator is trivially locally confluent
- claim1_full: Unique normal form is a fixed point (schedule-independent)
- corollary_complete_repair: Any fixed point w satisfies Φ(w) = 0

All proofs are sorry-free. Standard axioms only: propext, Classical.choice, Quot.sound.
